About this site

Contact: benjamin.bienz@colorado.edu

Science communication to the general public is often lacking. Papers are tedious and confusing even when the results are interesting and relevant. Articles tend to just list the results, making it hard for people to contextualize them.

This site is an example of how we can do better. Everyone knows exercise is good for you, but most people don't know exactly how it's good or how much they need to do. This discourages people from making a plan - they instead always feel guilty about not doing more.

By interactively linking hours of exercise to health outcomes, I hope this site motivates people to make a plan. The plot shows how even small amounts of activity translate to huge reductions in mortality from common diseases.
